JERRY'S JOURNEY IN WEB DEVELOPMENT

My Web development started in 2024 after being enrolled in Ghana Communication Technology University. After being enrolled, I was introduced to FreeCodecamp by my lecturer. Even though the journey hasn't being easy but hoping to get there as a web developer. Some weeks ago, Our lecturer asked us to host one of our project from freecodecamp. Eventually i went to research on the internet and found out some website that can help me host my website for free without any charges. i found this website called tinny website which i can say has made hosting of website easy and efficient. Look actually it hasnt been easy. i started by learning HTML then gradually came to Css then head to php,MySQl and Javascript. Recently am on Javascript and it has not been easy. Actually i cant give you any trick or secret that can help you practice coding. Infact coding will take your time, your money and everything because when you start it , it possesses you and everything.
